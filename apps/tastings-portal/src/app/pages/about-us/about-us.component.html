About Us

Our Story - A Celebration of Mexican Tradition

Our mission is to share the captivating essence of mezcal with the world. We are a team of dedicated mezcal enthusiasts who have traveled the length and breadth of Mexico, sourcing the finest spirits from small-batch producers and traditional family distilleries. Our mezcal tastings are designed to immerse you in the rich history, cultural significance, and complex flavors of this ancient spirit. We believe that every sip tells a story, and we are committed to providing you with an unparalleled experience that will ignite your passion for mezcal.
